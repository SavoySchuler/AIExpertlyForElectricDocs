# Version History

## v0.2 — Expanded Founder Review Draft

Status: Draft  
Prepared for: AIExpertly founder review  
Canonical format: Markdown

### Scope

This version expands the initial governance system into a fuller founder-review package for a Washington LLC with three equal founders, reverse vesting, domain-driven executive authority, founder departure protections, AI governance, and practical operating workflows.

### Major documents expanded

- Operating Agreement
- Founders Charter
- Governance Manual
- Executive Handbook
- Governance Playbook
- AI Governance Policy
- Information Security Policy
- Equity & Compensation Philosophy
- Governance Matrix
- Officer Roles Schedule
- Founder Roles Schedule
- Founder Expectations Schedule
- Initial Cap Table
- Reverse Vesting Schedule
- Pre-Existing IP Schedule
- Capital Contributions and Founder Loans Schedule
- Decision Journal Template

### Governance decisions reflected

- Washington LLC assumption.
- Equal founder ownership and voting power among three founders.
- 100,000,000 authorized Membership Units.
- Founder Units issued immediately, subject to reverse vesting.
- Four-year vesting, one-year cliff, monthly vesting thereafter.
- Ownership-based profit and loss allocation.
- Discretionary distributions with tax distribution and Excess Cash policy.
- CEO accountable for business execution and enterprise value.
- CTO accountable for technology execution, including technical architecture and AI implementation.
- Domain-driven executive authority for COO, CFO, CIO, CAIO, CPO, CXO, and CBO.
- CAIO reports to CTO unless otherwise approved.
- Protected Approval for major strategic decisions.
- Unanimous Approval for constitutional founder-rights changes.
- Notice requirements, voting windows, electronic voting, and silence-not-approval.
- Good Leaver and Bad Leaver structure.
- Founder abandonment process.
- Persistent Material Non-Performance process.
- Death and disability buyout protections.
- Company-owned IP, including AI assets, prompts, models, datasets, code, customer-related work product, and pre-formation work.
- Customer data restrictions.
- AI Assets Register.
- Financial controls and banking thresholds.
- Negotiation, mediation, arbitration, and shotgun deadlock process.
- Decision Journal for major decisions.
