## 3. Founder Equality Principle

The initial bargain is equal founder ownership, equal voting power, and ownership-based economic participation. No issuance, transfer, amendment, recapitalization, conversion, or incentive plan may be used to indirectly change that founding bargain except through the approval thresholds required in the Operating Agreement.

## 4. Fully Diluted Basis

Unless expressly stated otherwise, ownership percentages are measured on a fully diluted basis. Future equity pools, convertible instruments, profits interests, or other ownership-linked instruments should be modeled before approval.

## 5. Future Equity Incentive Plan Authorization

The Company may later establish an equity incentive plan of up to **10% of fully diluted ownership** only with the approval required by the Operating Agreement and Governance Matrix. No pool is created by this schedule.
