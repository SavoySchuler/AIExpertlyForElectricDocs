# Attorney Review Checklist

Version: v0.2 draft  
Status: Founder review / counsel review aid

> This checklist is intended to help a Washington business attorney, tax advisor, and startup counsel review the AIExpertly Governance System efficiently. It is not legal advice.

## 1. Formation and Entity Structure

- Confirm Washington LLC formation assumptions.
- Confirm registered agent, principal office, and filing requirements.
- Confirm whether the Company should remain a Washington LLC or convert before financing.
- Confirm whether any foreign qualification is required based on operations.
- Confirm whether the business purpose language is sufficiently broad.

## 2. Operating Agreement Enforceability

- Review the Operating Agreement for Washington LLC enforceability.
- Confirm amendment mechanics.
- Confirm electronic signature and notice provisions.
- Confirm whether any provisions should be moved to separate agreements.
- Confirm that schedules can be updated without accidentally amending core legal rights.

## 3. Founder Equity and Reverse Vesting

- Confirm 100,000,000 authorized Membership Units structure.
- Confirm three equal founders and initial one-third founder issuance structure.
- Confirm reverse vesting enforceability.
- Confirm four-year vesting, one-year cliff, and monthly vesting language.
- Confirm repurchase mechanics and exercise window.
- Confirm whether separate restricted unit purchase agreements are required.
- Confirm whether tax elections or protective filings are recommended.
- Confirm double-trigger acceleration language.

## 4. Tax and Accounting

- Confirm partnership tax treatment unless another election is intended.
- Confirm capital account language.
- Confirm tax distribution provision.
- Confirm treatment of founder loans.
- Confirm treatment of non-cash contributions.
- Confirm whether profits interests or other LLC equity incentives require special tax provisions.
- Confirm accounting method and fiscal year.

## 5. Governance and Voting

- Confirm Member Approval, Protected Approval, and Unanimous Approval definitions.
- Confirm the Governance Matrix does not conflict with the Operating Agreement.
- Confirm notice periods and voting windows.
- Confirm silence-not-approval language.
- Confirm emergency meeting provisions.
- Confirm whether quorum provisions are needed or should be expanded.
- Confirm conflict-of-interest and abstention treatment.

## 6. Officer Authority

- Review CEO, CTO, COO, CFO, CIO, CAIO, CPO, CXO, and CBO role definitions.
- Confirm domain-driven authority is legally coherent.
- Confirm officer appointment and removal provisions.
- Confirm officer fiduciary duties.
- Confirm CAIO reporting relationship and AI governance responsibilities.
- Confirm signing authority and banking controls.

## 7. Founder Departure and Buy-Sell

- Confirm Good Leaver and Bad Leaver definitions.
- Confirm abandonment process: 90 days non-performance, written notice, 30-day cure.
- Confirm Persistent Material Non-Performance treatment.
- Confirm death and disability provisions.
- Confirm heirs receive economic rights only unless admitted as Members.
- Confirm Fair Market Value appraisal process.
- Confirm buyout payment terms.
- Confirm Bad Leaver discount enforceability.
- Confirm shotgun deadlock clause enforceability.

## 8. Transfers, ROFR, Drag-Along, and Tag-Along

- Confirm transfer restrictions.
- Confirm Company and Member rights of first refusal.
- Confirm preemptive rights.
- Confirm drag-along and tag-along mechanics.
- Confirm treatment of transferees as economic holders versus voting Members.
- Confirm securities-law implications.

## 9. Intellectual Property

- Confirm present-assignment language.
- Confirm pre-formation work assignment.
- Confirm Pre-Existing IP Schedule treatment.
- Confirm treatment of code, prompts, prompt libraries, datasets, models, workflows, domains, brands, customer lists, documentation, and business assets.
- Confirm assignment or license of founder-owned assets.
- Confirm invention assignment tail after departure.
- Confirm confidentiality survival periods.

## 10. AI Governance and Customer Data

- Confirm customer data restrictions.
- Confirm model training restrictions.
- Confirm AI Assets Register obligations.
- Confirm model provenance documentation.
- Confirm treatment of third-party model and data licenses.
- Confirm open-source AI controls.
- Confirm regulated or sensitive AI use case escalation.
- Confirm security obligations for AI vendors and model providers.

## 11. Employment, Contractors, and Advisors

- Confirm employee and contractor invention assignment requirements.
- Confirm advisor equity treatment.
- Confirm contractor equity treatment.
- Confirm non-solicitation enforceability.
- Confirm no broad non-compete language.
- Confirm founder compensation approval process.

## 12. Dispute Resolution

- Confirm negotiation, mediation, arbitration, and emergency relief provisions.
- Confirm bad-faith fee shifting standard.
- Confirm venue, governing law, and arbitration rules.
- Confirm whether disputes involving IP, trade secrets, or injunctive relief need court carve-outs.
- Confirm shotgun clause and third-party sale fallback.

## 13. Insurance and Risk

- Confirm authorization for D&O, E&O, cyber liability, commercial general liability, and key-person insurance.
- Confirm approval thresholds for policies above premium limits.
- Confirm indemnification provisions if added or required.
- Confirm cybersecurity and incident response obligations.

## 14. Execution Package

Before signing, prepare or confirm:

- Final Operating Agreement.
- Initial Cap Table.
- Founder restricted unit or issuance agreements, if recommended.
- IP assignment agreements.
- Founder confidentiality agreements, if separate.
- Spousal consents, if applicable.
- Founder loan notes, if any.
- Pre-Existing IP Schedule.
- Initial officer appointments.
- Written consent approving formation actions.
- Bank authorization resolutions.
