## Purpose

The Decision Journal preserves institutional memory for major governance, strategy, financial, technical, AI, security, product, hiring, fundraising, and acquisition decisions.

The goal is not bureaucracy. The goal is to record why the Company made important decisions so that future founders, executives, attorneys, investors, and acquirers can understand the reasoning.

## When to Use This Template

Use this template for:

- Reserved Matters;
- Strategic Technology Decisions;
- fundraising decisions;
- acquisition offers;
- major customer data exceptions;
- major AI governance decisions;
- major security incidents;
- equity grants or equity pool changes;
- founder role changes;
- founder departure, buy-sell, or repurchase decisions;
- annual strategic plan approval;
- annual operating plan approval;
- material pivots.
