## 1. Purpose

This template supports the semi-annual Founder Review process. The review is intended to clarify expectations, identify support needs, improve accountability, and update founder roles without weaponizing vested equity.

Performance concerns may affect officer role, compensation, support, future grants, or responsibilities. They do not affect vested ownership unless the conduct separately qualifies as Cause, Abandonment, or another Bad Leaver event under the Operating Agreement.
